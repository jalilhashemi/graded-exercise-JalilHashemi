Project Description

With this light version of socrative a teacher can create very easily a queation with as many answer as it is necessary for that question.

Once the question with its possible answers are created, the students can choose the right answer or answers in form of multiple choice.

The teacher can see the result of the chosen answer in a separate page called report. The report page illustrate the created possible answers with their progress-bar which shows how many student have attended and which possible answers were chosen by students and how many.
